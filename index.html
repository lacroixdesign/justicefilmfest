Justice Film Festival

The Justice Film Festival is an annual event dedicated showcasing the inspiring stories of vulnerable and oppressed people worldwide. It is a platform for conversation and connection amongst justice-minded individuals and is the only event of its kind in North America.

Our team hand selects films from both well-known and emerging filmmakers. These films move and inspire the aspirational community one that seeks to live out justice in a beautiful but broken world.

We strive to curate a festival that is diverse in both content and geography. The collection of justice films offer new perspectives on topics well known and reveals new stories of unexpected courage and redemption.

We believe that telling stories through art and film can shape global culture for the common good.
